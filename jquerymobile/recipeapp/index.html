Pasta with Tomato and Basil

Back

Traditional yet simple pasta dish with tomato sauce and basil.

Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack pasta
  • 30ml olive oil
  • 1kg fresh tomatoes
  • handful of fresh basil
  • 3-5 cloves minced garlic
  • tomato-based pasta sauce
  • red chili flakes (optional)
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Chop and quarter the tomatoes
  2. Heat oil and add garlic and add chili
  3. Add tomatoes and stir until tomatoes thicken
  4. Add basil, and salt and pepper to taste
  5. When sauce is cooked, remove from heat
  6. Cook the pasta until al dente
  7. Mix the sauce into the pasta. Enjoy!
